     The idea for 'Awhile Since' was tumbling around the mind of Roop Bassi since 2011. He had been very interested in improving himself and increasing his quality of life so he enjoyed apps and websites that helped you do that. He noticed that he was happiest when he had a routine filled with activities he genuinely enjoyed doing, but he was very busy and would often forget to do the things (even the simple things) that made him happy. He wanted an easy way to be reminded of these activities, whether it be something like taking a medication, having sushi, or going skydiving.

     He envisioned a website to help out with all of this as well as introduce him to new activities and to see what activities other people want. He met up software developer Marty Corbett who developed the site and helped to bring his vision to life.

     Roop enjoys long walks on the beach, playing spike ball, movies in the park, and hosts segway tours in his free time. He is truly one of the best people you could hope to meet. Feel free to contact him with feedback about your experience on the site as well as suggestions for ways to make it better.
